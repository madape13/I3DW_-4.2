This pack is a small version of the "Substance Materials" pack. These materials has properties that you can change in the inspector panel.You can also change the resolution from 32x32 up to 2048x2048 with real quality changes.

You can use the same material in different parts of your scene and it will look unique in each one.

In order to know how to use these materials deeply, watch the official video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FvLsw1Cy4Lg

If you need a new material you can ask me and i will do my best to make it. If you have any problems, doubts or sugestions, please, let me know.

Thank you!
